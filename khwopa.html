KHWOPA ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Affiliated to Purbanchal University.Estd. 2001

(Dedicated To Country & People)

KhEC is Nepal's first community-based engineering college undertaken by Bhaktapur Municipality. It is centrally located at culturallu rich city Bhaktapur. KhEC will in every regard be the right destination for those who aspire to become professionals engineers and architects at affordable fees.

With a distant vision of maintaining Bhaktapur's Hard-won glory,the college aims to produce highly skilled engineers/architects that will have blends of both indigenous and modern day technologies.
